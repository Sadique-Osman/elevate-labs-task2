# elevate-labs-task2
# Data Visualization & Storytelling

## Objective
Build an interactive sales dashboard using Power BI to analyze 
Superstore sales data and derive actionable business insights.

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Dataset: Sample - Superstore.csv

## Dashboard Components
- KPI Cards: Total Sales (2.30M), Total Profit (286.40K), 
  Profit Margin (12%)
- Sales Trend Line Chart (2014-2017)
- Sales by Category Bar Chart
- Profit by Category Donut Chart
- Sales by Segment Donut Chart
- Profit by City Map
- Region & Sub-Category Slicers

## Key Business Insights
- Technology is the highest revenue category at $836K
- Consumer segment dominates with 50.56% of total sales
- Corporate segment contributes 30.74%
- Sales show consistent growth peaking in 2016-2017
- Office Supplies has lowest sales but steady demand
- West and East regions are most profitable
